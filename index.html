Fiona The Hippo

Meet Fiona the hippo - a cultural icon and arguably the best thing to happen in 2017. Fiona is a baby hippo who blessed us with her presence at the Cincinnati Zoo earlier this year. Aside from being sassy and adorable, Fiona has gone viral in multiple memes, and continues to bring joy.
